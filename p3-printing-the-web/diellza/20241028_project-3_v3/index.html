Portable Measuring Tape Tool

01   Unlock the Tape
Begin by releasing the tape lock if it’s engaged. Most portable tape measures have a locking mechanism that prevents the tape from retracting automatically.

02   Grip the Tape
Hold the housing of the tape measure firmly in one hand, making sure the tape itself can slide in and out freely.

03   Place the End Hook
At one end of the measuring tape, there is a small metal hook designed to latch onto objects. Secure the hook to the edge or starting point of the object you are measuring.

04   Ensure Stability
Make sure the hook is stable and won't slip off, which could affect the accuracy of your measurement.
